class  blaze::StrictlyLowerProxy< MT >
 Access proxy for strictly lower triangular matrices.The StrictlyLowerProxy provides controlled access to the elements of a non-const strictly lower triangular matrix. It guarantees that the strictly lower matrix invariant is not violated, i.e. that elements on the diagonal and in the upper part of the matrix remain 0.
